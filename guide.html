Here's our little guide

The first aspect you shuld be considering while playing cdda is combat here's a little list of what each weapon stat means:

  • Bash dmg: Blunt force damage of your weapon, the smash is pretty good at damaging through highly armored enemies.
  • Cut Dmg: Quick and powerful cuts, but tends to be less effective against armor.
  • Pierce Dmg: The best at piercing armor but strikes tend to be less powerful.
  • To Hit: Your chance of hitting the monster, in a few words: your presicion.
  • Techniques: At the start of the game you won't be able to get much from this, for now just ignore it.
  • Moves per attack: The amounts of moves consumed for each attack.
  • Damage/Turn:Basically your dps
